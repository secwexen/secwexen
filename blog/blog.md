# Blog

## Welcome to My Cybersecurity Journey

This blog is dedicated to documenting my ongoing exploration of cybersecurity, ethical hacking, and technology research. Here, I share insights, tutorials, tool experiments, and CTF experiences, with the goal of helping both beginners and advanced practitioners understand real-world security challenges.

### What You Will Find Here

* **Security Tutorials**: Step-by-step guides on penetration testing, vulnerability analysis, malware reverse engineering, and more.
* **Tool Experiments**: Reviews, usage, and custom scripts for popular tools such as Flipper Zero, HackRF, Proxmark3, WiFi Pineapple, and Raspberry Pi labs.
* **CTF Write-ups**: Capture The Flag challenge solutions, analysis, and techniques to sharpen your skills.
* **Research Notes**: Findings from personal experiments, OSINT investigations, threat intelligence research, and security protocol analysis.
* **Ethical Hacking Practices**: Emphasizing responsible testing, non-destructive experiments, and safe lab setups.

### My Approach

I aim to combine hands-on practice with continuous learning. Each post will focus on practical skills while keeping ethical considerations front and center. My goal is to make complex topics accessible and actionable for anyone interested in cybersecurity.

### Join the Journey

Follow along for updates, technical insights, and experiments in cybersecurity. Whether you are a student, professional, or hobbyist, this blog is meant to inspire curiosity, learning, and ethical exploration of technology.
